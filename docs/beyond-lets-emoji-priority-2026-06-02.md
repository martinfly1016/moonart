## Executive Takeaway

The next work should prioritize existing ranking improvements over broad new page expansion.

The highest-leverage opportunities are keywords already ranking around positions 2-7 with meaningful impressions. Moving these terms into the top 1-3 positions should produce faster traffic gains than creating many new pages from zero.
