### Semrush / Language Expansion Assessment

A Semrush session was restored through the user-authorized browser session. The work used Semrush keyword overview data to reassess Chinese and Korean localization potential.

Important Semrush observations:

#### Traditional Chinese / Taiwan


#### Korean

```text
이모티콘             KR volume 90.5K, KD 51%
카카오톡 이모티콘     KR volume 18.1K
하트 특수문자         KR volume 14.8K
카톡 이모티콘         KR volume 3.6K, KD 35%
특수문자             local volume unavailable, global volume 3.3K, related keyword pool about 4.6K
```

Decision:

- Korean has stronger raw keyword volume than Traditional Chinese.
- However, Korean would require deeper local context, especially KakaoTalk-oriented positioning.
- Traditional Chinese has more precise copy-tool queries but smaller volume.
- The expected short-term ROI is not high enough to start either localization track now.

Current decision:

- Do not start `/ko/`, `/zh-hant/`, or `/zh-hans/` yet.
- Keep the idea parked.
- Continue improving existing Japanese and English tools first.
- Revisit localization if GSC/Semrush later shows clearer demand or if there is capacity for a proper Korean/KakaoTalk content angle.
