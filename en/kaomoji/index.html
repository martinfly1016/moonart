FAQ

Can I use these kaomoji on Instagram, Discord, TikTok, and Slack?

Yes. Kaomoji are text characters, so you can paste them into most apps that accept normal text. Some symbols may look slightly different depending on the device and font.

Can I copy more than one kaomoji at a time?

Yes. Click several kaomoji to add them to the draft, then copy the full draft in one action.

What is the difference between kaomoji and emoji?

Kaomoji are built from text characters, while emoji are colored pictographs. Kaomoji blend into text more naturally and are useful when you want a softer, more expressive tone.

Do these work on mobile?

Yes. On mobile, the copy draft is available from the bottom sheet so you can keep browsing and copy when ready.
