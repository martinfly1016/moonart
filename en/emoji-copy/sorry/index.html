How to use sorry emoji

Sorry emoji help soften short messages. 🙏 can mean please, thanks, or sorry depending on context. 🙇 feels more formal or apologetic, while 😔 and 😣 make a short message feel gentler.

Light apology

Use 🙏, 😣, or 🥺 for casual chat replies when the message should stay soft.

More formal apology

Use bowing emoji when a request or apology needs to feel more respectful.

Thanks after sorry

Folded hands and handshake emoji can also support a thank-you message after an apology.
