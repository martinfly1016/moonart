How to use warning emoji

Warning emoji help important text stand out. ⚠️ works for general caution, 🚨 suggests urgency, ⛔ and 🚫 indicate prohibition, and ❗ or ‼️ add emphasis.

Notices

Use caution and exclamation emoji before short instructions or reminders.

Rules

No-entry, stop, and prohibited marks work well for rule lists and chat guidelines.

Checks

Use ✅ beside warning symbols when you need to show a completed or allowed item.
